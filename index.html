What GroupMind Does

Everything your group project needs

Stop juggling five apps. GroupMind brings your whole team into one AI-powered workspace.

🧠

Smart Task Division

GroupMind analyzes each member's schedule, strengths, and availability, then splits work into fair, manageable tasks. No more arguing over who does what.

💬

Built-In Team Chat

One unified chat for your group — no more losing updates across iMessage, Discord, and email. Threaded discussions, file sharing, and AI-summarized conversations keep everyone up to speed.

📅

Meeting Finder

Connect your calendars and GroupMind instantly finds times that work for the entire team. One click to schedule, automatic reminders, and built-in video call links.

📊

Project Health Score

A live dashboard showing how on-track your project is — task completion, days until deadline, participation balance, and a predicted final quality score.

✍️

AI Writing Assistant

Stuck on an outline? Need to merge five people's writing into one voice? GroupMind's AI drafts, edits, and combines sections without losing the grade-worthy content your team produced.

🔔

Accountability Nudges

Friendly (but persistent) reminders go directly to teammates who are falling behind, so you never have to be the awkward one sending "hey, did you finish your part?" texts.

🔗

App Integrations

Works with Google Docs, Canvas, Notion, Slack, GitHub, and your university LMS. Sync deadlines from your syllabus in seconds.

⚖️

Conflict Resolution AI

Disagreements happen. GroupMind mediates — summarizing both sides, suggesting compromises, and keeping the conversation productive so the work keeps moving.

How It Works

From assignment to A+ in four steps

Set up your project in under two minutes. GroupMind handles the coordination from there.

01

Create Your Group

Invite teammates with a link or email. Connect your calendars and set your deadline.

02

AI Divides the Work

GroupMind breaks the project into tasks and assigns them based on everyone's skills and schedule.

03

Collaborate in Real Time

Chat, share files, hold AI-assisted meetings, and track everyone's progress in one place.
